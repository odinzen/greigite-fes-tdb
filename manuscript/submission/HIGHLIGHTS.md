# Highlights

(Per journal requirements: mandatory, separate editable file, 3–5 bullets, ≤85 characters incl. spaces each. Counts verified below.)

- Open, reproducible CALPHAD workflow adds measured greigite data to an Fe–S(–O) TDB
- Greigite (Fe₃S₄) is a thermodynamically stable thiospinel, not metastable
- Greigite preempts pyrrhotite; decomposition margin +19.5 ± 7.9 kJ/mol (+2.5σ)
- Greigite is the sulfur analogue of magnetite, the Fe₃X₄ spinel of the redox ladder
- Suppressing greigite recovers the classical pyrrhotite–pyrite Fe–S diagram
